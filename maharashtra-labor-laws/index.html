Maharashtra just overhauled labor laws.
10-hour days, 60-hour weeks, and better pay for overtime.

The Big Changes

⏰ Daily Work Hours

Private Sector 9 → 10 hours
Factory Workers 9 → 12 hours

📅 Weekly Limits

Work Week 60 hours
Quarterly Limit 125 → 144 hours

The Fine Print

✅ Worker Protections

  • 📝 Written consent required for overtime
  • 💰 Paid overtime for extra hours
  • 🏖️ 2 paid days off after 60-hour week
  • ☕ 6-hour continuous work limit (up from 5)

🏢 Business Benefits

  • 📈 More operational flexibility
  • 💼 Easier for small businesses (<20 employees)
  • 🚫 No Gumasta Licence required for small firms
  • 🎯 Attract more investment to Maharashtra
