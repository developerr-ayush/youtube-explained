✅ Getting Cheaper

  • • Daily essentials (soaps, food) → 5%
  • • Healthcare & education → 5%/Nil
  • • Small cars & appliances → 18%
  • • Agriculture equipment → 5%

📈 Getting Costlier

  • • Tobacco & cigarettes → 40%
  • • Luxury cars → 40%
  • • Aerated drinks → 40%
  • • Online gaming → 40%
